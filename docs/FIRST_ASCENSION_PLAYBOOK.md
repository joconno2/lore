# First-ascension playbook — FAIR PLAY (no wizard teleport/wish)

Mined from our KB (wiki, Maniac's/Mikko guides, NetHack source, BotHack — the one bot
in our KB that ascended). This is the real-play path an agent (on AutoAscend) must
follow: descend from DL1 on its own, assemble the kit through play, reach Gehennom
strong. AutoAscend dies ~DL10-17 today; the diagnosis and fix are below. NO wizard
mode anywhere.

## 0. Why the agent dies at DL10-17 (the whole point)

DL10-17 = the lower Gnomish Mines + the deep descent toward Medusa. The KB is
unambiguous: chars die there not from descending too fast but from being in that band
**without poison resistance / reflection / magic resistance and without the
Elbereth-and-retreat reflexes.** Fix = the prep gate (s5) + instadeath avoidance (s4)
+ survival reflexes (s3), not "descend slower."

## 1. The route + depth/XL geography (macro plan)

| Milestone | DL | Gate |
|---|---|---|
| Gnomish Mines entrance | 2-4 | recon first level only, grab gear, retreat |
| Oracle | 5-9 | — |
| **Sokoban** (up from Oracle) | 6-10 | do BEFORE deep Mines; prize = bag OR amulet-of-reflection (50/50, pick one) |
| Minetown | 5-8 (mid-Mines) | RESOURCE HUB: BUC-ID altar, price-ID, buy protection, stash |
| Mines' End (luckstone) | 10-13 | guaranteed luckstone; lower Mines is the trap gauntlet |
| Quest portal | 11-16 | **needs XL14** |
| Medusa | 21-28 | reflection or self-blind mandatory vs gaze |
| Castle (wand of wishing) | 25-29 | MR mandatory; guaranteed wand-of-wishing chest |
| Gehennom entrance (Valley) | 26-30 | the prep gate (s5) must be met |

Route order (BotHack full-explore): Minetown → Sokoban → (Excalibur once armored) →
Mines' End luckstone → Quest → Medusa → Castle wand → complete kit → Gehennom.

## 2. XP / leveling — the XL14 wall

- XL gates: XL3 sacrifice-gift eligible; **XL5 lawful → dip for Excalibur**; XL12 →
  teleport-at-will; **XL14 → Quest (hard gate).**
- XP doubles per level (XL14 = 80k) while monster XP rises slowly → grinding stalls.
  Cross XL10→14 via wraith corpses (+1 level each) / gain-level potions, not grinding.
- **Over-leveling COSTS:** higher XL → harder/larger monster spawns, pricier protection.
  Target XL14, NOT XL20+. Level to the gate, no further.

## 3. Survival fundamentals (the reflexes reckless play lacks)

**Prayer = emergency backstop only.** Initial timeout 300 (safe to pray turn 301).
With MAJOR trouble (HP≤1/7 or ≤5, Weak/Fainting, stoning, lethal illness) an emergency
prayer works ~turn 101 (timeout<201). NEVER pray with negative align/Luck, god angry,
in Gehennom, or on a cross-aligned altar. Over-praying backfires (LORE-confirmed). The
"~1000-turn cooldown" folklore is wrong — the rule is timeout-vs-trouble.

**Elbereth** (dust `E`-`-`-"Elbereth", lands in one turn, ~73% success): stand-still
melee/directed-spell defense. Breaks when you act (melee corrupts 3 chars). Does NOT
work in Gehennom, vs @/A/minotaurs/Riders/blind monsters/the Wizard. **Pre-engrave on
stairs/chokepoints; fight from the upstairs; retreat to it to heal.**

**Retreat at HP<half, not at 1 HP.** Heal by resting near/on the upstairs.

**Food:** carry non-rotting reserves (lizard/lichen corpses — never rot, never poison;
lizard also cures stoning) + rations. Never eat kobold/old corpses (poison/food-pois).
Eat safe fresh corpses to Satiated (not past → choke). AA already eats at NOT_HUNGRY;
the gap is SUPPLY, not timing.

## 4. Instadeath monsters — NEVER melee these

- **Floating eye 'e':** melee → passive paralysis up to 127 turns → nibbled to death.
  Ranged-kill, apply a mirror, or skip. Immune only w/ free action/reflection/blind.
- **Cockatrice/chickatrice 'c':** touch/melee-barehand/eat/pick-up corpse → petrify.
  **Wear GLOVES** near them (then the corpse is a safe stoning weapon). Carry a lizard
  corpse to cure stoning-in-progress.
- **Mind flayer 'h' (magenta):** can spawn in the Mines out-of-depth; brain-drain →
  brainlessness instadeath (life-saving does NOT avert). Farlook magenta 'h'; wear a
  helm in the Mines (blocks 7/8 tentacles); ranged/wand-of-death only, never melee.
- **Medusa:** gaze petrifies w/o reflection or self-blind.
- **Gas spore / exploding 'e':** explode on death — don't melee adjacent.
- **Drawbridge/moat/lava (Castle, quest):** kill more than the bosses (~5.6x). Never
  strike/open a drawbridge while standing in front of it.

## 5. The PREP GATE — "strong and prepared" (the structural goal AA lacks)

Do NOT descend past the checkpoints without the gear (prayer & Elbereth are OFF in
Gehennom, so these must be carried):
- **before lower Mines / Medusa:** poison resistance.
- **before Castle / Gehennom:** MAGIC RESISTANCE (gray DSM / cloak of MR / Magicbane —
  gear only, can't pray for it) + REFLECTION (amulet / silver DSM / shield of
  reflection) + FREE ACTION (ring / wielded Excalibur) + good AC + speed + escape.
- Hard gates before Gehennom: (MR ∧ reflection ∧ unicorn-horn ∧ free-action) and
  (blessed luckstone ∧ Luck≥0).

## 6. Ascension kit — checklist + FAIR-PLAY source

MUST-HAVE: magic resistance (GDSM/cloak-MR); reflection (SDSM/amulet/shield); blessed
unicorn horn (kill a unicorn — cures all status, "absolute must"); blessed luckstone
(Mines' End, guaranteed); free action (ring or Excalibur); poison res (eat safe
corpses); DSM body armor; artifact/silver weapon; AC ≤ -10.

Sources in play:
- **Luckstone** — guaranteed Mines' End (DL10-13).
- **Bag of holding OR amulet of reflection** — Sokoban prize (ONE only, 50/50). Take
  the amulet if you have no other reflection; else the bag.
- **Excalibur** — dip long sword in a fountain at XL≥5 (free action while wielded).
- **Artifact weapon** — altar sacrifice gift (first gift is co-aligned, guaranteed).
- **Wand of wishing** — guaranteed Castle chest (DL25-29); +charging+wrest = 5-7 wishes.
- **Protection (AC)** — donate 400×XL gold to the co-aligned Minetown priest; buy 2-3
  points cheaply at low XL (BotHack gate: protection<3 AND XL<15). Best early AC move.
- **Unicorn horn / free action ring / speed boots / life saving** — drops/shops/wish.
- **DSM** — kill a dragon → scales → enchant-armor → mail; or wish.

Altar: drop items to BUC-ID (amber=blessed, black=cursed, none=uncursed); sacrifice
fresh co-aligned corpses for gifts (needs timeout 0, align≥0, XL≥3, Luck≥0) — also
raises Luck. Use the Minetown temple as the BUC-ID + protection + sacrifice hub.

## 7. Fair-play wish ORDER (Castle wand — BotHack's tested cascade)

Syntax always: `blessed greased fixed +2 <item>` (never omit "scale" on DSM).
1. `2 scrolls of charging` (unlock the wand) → 2. ring of levitation (below Medusa) →
3. genocide `;`+electric eel → 4. **gray DSM (MR)** or 5. **silver DSM (reflection)**
(whichever property you lack) → 6. shield of reflection (if DSM but no reflection) →
7. remove curse → 8. genocide L/`;` → 9. 7 wax candles → 10. speed boots → 11. genocide
mind flayers → 12. helm of telepathy → 13. wand of death → 14. enchant armor to AC≤-21
→ 15. amulet of life saving → spare wishes: life-saving / wand of death / enchant armor.
One-line priority: **DSM(MR) → reflection → speed → life-saving → bag (if no Soko) →
artifact weapon (unless sacrificing).**

## 8. What to encode as agent policy (the build targets)

1. **Macro route + prep gate:** Mines-recon → Sokoban → Minetown(BUC/protection/stash)
   → Mines'End(luckstone) → gate deep descent on resistances, not depth.
2. **Instadeath-avoidance reflexes:** never melee floating-eye/cockatrice-barehand/mind
   -flayer; wear helm in Mines + gloves near 'c'; carry lizard corpse.
3. **Kit-acquisition goals** (BotHack `desired-items`/`wish`/`get-protection`/altar):
   Sokoban prize, Minetown protection, Mines'End luckstone, altar BUC + sacrifice,
   Excalibur dip, Castle wand → wish cascade.
4. **Survival:** prayer as emergency-only (timeout rule), pre-engrave Elbereth on
   stairs, retreat at HP<half, non-rotting food supply.
5. **Level to XL14, not beyond.**
